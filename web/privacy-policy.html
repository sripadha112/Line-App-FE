Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026 | Effective Date: April 19, 2026

NeextApp ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

For All Users:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, age, gender
  • Contact Information: Mobile number, email address
  • Location Information: Address, city, state, PIN code, country
  • Account Credentials: Encrypted authentication tokens (we do NOT store passwords)

For Patients:

  • Medical Information:
    • Health vital signs (height, weight, blood pressure, heart rate, body temperature, blood oxygen level)
    • Blood group
    • Medical conditions (diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease)
    • Current medications and drug allergies
    • Medical history and family medical history
    • Past surgical history
  • Appointment Information: Appointment dates, times, doctor details, appointment notes
  • Prescription Information: Prescribed medicines, dosages, medical notes from doctors
  • Family Member Information: Names and basic health information of family members you add

For Doctors:

  • Professional Information: Medical specialization, designation, registration/license number
  • Workplace Details: Clinic/hospital names, addresses, contact numbers
  • Practice Information: Consultation hours, availability, appointment scheduling preferences

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, pages viewed, time spent, navigation patterns
  • Location Data: GPS location (when you search for nearby doctors)
  • Push Notification Data: FCM device tokens, notification delivery status

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate appointment booking between patients and doctors
  • Enable doctors to view patient medical information during consultations
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Generate and store medical prescriptions

Medical Information Use:

Your medical information is used ONLY for:

  • ✓ Displaying to doctors with whom you have booked appointments
  • ✓ Generating your medical prescriptions
  • ✓ Maintaining your health record history

We will NEVER:

  • ✗ Sell your medical information to third parties
  • ✗ Share your medical data for advertising purposes
  • ✗ Use your health data for unrelated purposes

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Healthcare Providers

Patient Medical Data Shared with Doctors: When you book an appointment, the doctor can access your contact information, medical history, vital signs, current medications, allergies, and prescriptions they have issued to you.

Doctor Information Shared with Patients: Patients can see doctor's name, specialization, clinic details, availability, and public profile information.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party providers:

  • Cloud Hosting: Supabase/PostgreSQL for secure database storage
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • SMS Services: OTP delivery for authentication

4. Data Storage and Security

Security Measures:

  • ✓ Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • ✓ Access Controls: Role-based access restrictions
  • ✓ Authentication: Secure OTP-based authentication
  • ✓ Database Security: Protected against SQL injection
  • ✓ API Security: JWT token-based authentication

Data Retention:

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • Medical Records: Retained for legal/medical record-keeping (up to 7 years)
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 90 days

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • ✓ Access: View all your personal and medical information
  • ✓ Update: Correct inaccurate information
  • ✓ Delete: Request deletion of your account
  • ✓ Export: Download your medical records and prescriptions
  • ✓ Opt-Out: Disable push notifications

Account Deletion:

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, OR
  2. Contact us at support@neextapp.com

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is NOT intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are 13-17, you must have parental/guardian consent.

7. International Data Transfers

Our primary servers are located in India. Data may be transferred to other countries with adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

European Users (GDPR): You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port data, object to processing, and withdraw consent.

California Users (CCPA): You have rights to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data), and non-discrimination.

9. Healthcare Data Compliance

We comply with:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)
  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)
  • Medical Council of India telemedicine guidelines
  • HIPAA-inspired practices

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via email, push notification, or prominent notice in the app. Changes become effective immediately for minor clarifications, or after 30 days notice for material changes.
