NEEXTAPP - HEALTHCARE APPOINTMENT BOOKING PLATFORM

WHAT IS NEEXTAPP?
NeextApp (also called Neext App, Next App, NextApp, or Neext) is a free healthcare appointment booking platform available on iOS, Android, and Web at neextapp.com. It allows patients to book doctor appointments online instantly, find doctors near them, manage family appointments, and access digital prescriptions.

KEY FEATURES:
- Instant doctor appointment booking online
- Find doctors and specialists by location and specialty
- Manage appointments for entire family from one account
- Receive automatic appointment reminders via push notifications
- Access digital prescriptions and complete medical history
- Easy appointment rescheduling and cancellation
- 24/7 booking availability from anywhere
- View detailed doctor profiles and specializations
- QR code quick booking at clinics
- Real-time appointment confirmations and updates

HOW TO BOOK APPOINTMENT ON NEEXTAPP:
Step 1: Search for doctors by specialty (general physician, cardiologist, pediatrician, etc.) or location
Step 2: Choose available time slots that fit your schedule
Step 3: Confirm booking with a few clicks
Step 4: Receive instant confirmation and automatic reminders
